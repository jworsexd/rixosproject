About The Beta

Discover the World of Rixos

Rixos is a hand-crafted 2D adventure currently in closed beta. We are actively shaping the game with our community's feedback. Every tester helps us build something magical.

Play as a curious hero exploring mysterious environments, from candle-lit cottages to enchanted forests. Every corner hides a secret.

  • Hand-painted 2D environments
  • Atmospheric adaptive soundtrack
  • Intuitive puzzle mechanics
  • Multiple story branches
  • Community-driven development

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the beta.

How do I join the closed beta?
Simply click the Download button and enter your email. The beta installer will start automatically. No key required for now.
Will my progress carry over to the full release?
Beta progress will not carry over to the 1.0 release. We are constantly rebalancing and restructuring content based on feedback.
Where can I report bugs or give feedback?
Join our Discord community! We have dedicated channels for bug reports, suggestions, and general feedback.
Is the beta available on Mac or Linux?
Currently, the beta is Windows-only. Mac and Linux support is planned for a future update closer to the full release.
